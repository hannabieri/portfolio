Vote & Vous

The "Tinder" of the European elections: a tool to help you find out your political match for the European parliament elections in 2019.

My role Concept • Design
Context Side project

Design • Corporate Identity & Branding

The corporate design and logo of Vote&Vous should embody the core values of Vote&Vous. This project aims to inform citizens neutrally and thoughtfully so that they can form an opinion for the election objectively. Vote&Vous wants to bring transparency into the political debate and has as a goal to drive the citizens to participate in the election (participation tool). The principles of democracy are at the heart of the project: political education, openness, participation, information, transparency, reason, objectivity, and neutrality.

Vote&Vous’s corporate design and logo should reflect these principles.

Colours

The European yellow plus a fresh blue tone have a contemporary & refreshing effect. By adjusting the blue tone a not too strong European association and easy differentiation is achieved, nevertheless, the colour values are still very close to the EU colours and therefore easy to "understand".

Logo

The two squares represent the two elements "Vote&Vous", participation and information and thus also for the core values of V&V. These can also be abstractly seen as ballot boxes, the box and ballot. This could also be used in the future, for example, as a logo animation. Thus, an association to Vote&Vous and even an active invitation for the actual voting participation gets created, but in a very subtle way. Through abstraction, the values of clarity and transparency are put into focus. By preserving the font of the previous logo, we grant a recognition value. This form of the logo is timeless and will appear modern over a long period. 

The logo is a word-picture mark, but both the word mark and the figurative mark work independently of each other, as well as assembled as a word-picture mark.
