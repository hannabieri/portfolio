Diskutier Mit Mir

Diskutier Mit Mir bursts bubbles of opinion and promotes the exchange between people who think differently on the internet.

My role UI Design • Campaign 
Context Part-time employed

Campaign

For the local elections in Germany 2019, the focus was on developing a new visual language, which both fits in with the design of the application and our Corporate Design, but also transmits a fresh, rejuvenated look. It was important to us to develop a unique style for share pics, posters, events, roll-ups, …, so Diskutier Mit Mir can get recognized by its look and trigger curiosity through its appearance.
Additionally, it's crucial to get known as something own, not as a party, not as a political campaign, not as a messenger app, but as its own, political dialogue platform. To support the own look, we've often used own pictures, videos and drawings, instead of using some stock photos. That way, it was possible to create an own look even better.

Advertisement

Another part of the campaign were ads for our media partners and media cooperations. With a banner, the biggest challenge is to catch one's attention within seconds and to transfer an idea of what it is about without using too many words. With a reduced style and sketched elements, I have created banners which allow a reader to grasp the core of the project, the dialogue, and at the same time to create an interest for the platform.
