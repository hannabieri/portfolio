THE background

During my apprenticeship as a print media designer, I came in touch with print design for the first time. During the four years of training, my skill set, as well as my love for print design, grew. 

The beauty of the texture of a fitting paper, the accuracy of colour nuances in printing and the joy of holding a finished print product in your hand are priceless.

Additionally, after finishing my apprenticeship, I worked in the printing industry for some time before I moved onto the digital field and new adventures and challenges. But still today, I am thrilled to design print products from time to time or as a haptic addition to a digital product from flyers to tote bags, from stickers to posters.

I’ll showcase a couple of selected projects here, for any further details or works don’t hesitate to get in touch with me.
